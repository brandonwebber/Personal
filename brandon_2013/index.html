Profile

Writer and strategist working for Lift Interactive in Edmonton Alberta. I like exploring storytelling in digital mediums and publishing strategies for small to medium platforms.

I’m 1/2 of the Monogram team and the organizer for CreativeMornings/Edmonton.

I’m a runner, cyclist, bicycle builder, casual leatherworker, gardener and hobby short filmmaker and I’m happily married to the lovely Lydia Webber.
